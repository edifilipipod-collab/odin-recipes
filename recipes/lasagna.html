A recipe for the world's best lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a classic, comforting baked pasta dish built in rich, satisfying layers. Tender sheets of pasta are stacked with a slow-simmered meat sauce infused with garlic, tomatoes, and herbs, then balanced with a creamy ricotta filling and plenty of melted mozzarella. As it bakes, the flavors meld together into a hearty, cohesive dish with a bubbling, golden top and a soft, flavorful interior. It’s the kind of meal that feels both rustic and indulgent—perfect for a family dinner or any occasion that calls for something warm, filling, and deeply satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a pan over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Add the tomato sauce, season with salt and pepper, and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg and a pinch of salt.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom. Add a layer of lasagna noodles, then spread some of the ricotta mixture, followed by more meat sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella.
  5. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for about 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 10–15 minutes until the cheese is melted and slightly golden.
  7. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
